ABOUT US

Kaslyn is a modern modeling and talent agency dedicated to discovering, developing, and representing exceptional talent. Founded with a vision to redefine industry standards, Kaslyn connects creativity, confidence, and professionalism to create lasting impact in fashion, media, and advertising.

OUR SERVICES

HIGH-FASHION & EDITORIAL

Our models are available for fashion campaigns, runway shows, lookbooks, magazine editorials, and designer showcases. We work closely with brands, designers, and creative teams to deliver the right talent for every project.

COMMERCIAL & ADVERTISING

We provide models for commercials, product campaigns, lifestyle shoots, e-commerce, and corporate advertising. Our talent represents a wide range of ages, looks, and markets to meet diverse brand needs.

INFLUENCER & BRAND COLLABOATIONS

We connect models and digital creators with brands for influencer campaigns, social media promotions, and content creation. We manage partnerships that align with both brand identity and model image.

PORTFOLIO & TEST SHOOT COORDINATION

We coordinate professional test shoots, comp cards, and portfolio updates in collaboration with experienced photographers, stylists, makeup artists, and creative directors.

CASTING & TALENT BOOKING

We offer casting services for photographers, brands, production houses, and agencies. From shortlisting to final selection, we streamline the process to ensure efficient and reliable talent booking.

TALENT SCOUTING & DEVELOPMENT

We discover and develop new faces with strong potential. Our development program includes portfolio building, test shoots, runway training, posing techniques, branding guidance, and industry education.
